Instructions:

  • Place your bullets in the text box above
  • If your bullets are longer than 115 characters, they will highlight in red       
  • If your bullets are shorter than 112 characters, they will highlight in green
  • Bullets between 112 and 115 characters are left alone
  • Items highlighted in yellow        are where I think there might be duplicate verbs
  • Acronyms appear in blue       
  • If you have entered unicode characters (thin spaces) it will warn you and attempt to fix this for you
  • Highlight a word and right click to bring up a thesaurus. If you select a word, it will replace it in the text for you
