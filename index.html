Instructions:

  • Place your bullets in the text box above
  • Set your desired lengths above the text box
  • If your bullets too long, they will highlight in red       
  • If your bullets too short, they will highlight in green
  • Bullets that are between your minimum and maximum are left alone
  • Items highlighted in yellow        might be duplicate verbs
  • Acronyms appear in blue       
  •  
  • If you have entered unicode characters (thin spaces) it will warn you
  •  
  • Highlight a word and right click to bring up a thesaurus. If you select a word, it will replace it in the text for you
  • If you are wanting to use right click like you normally do, hold CTRL while you right click

Abbreviations *experimental*

  • Formatting matters here. See example to the right
  • Your abbreviatios must be in the following format:
  • No headings
  • Longform words on the left, shorthand words on the right
  • Alphabetized; it will replace words from top-to-bottom in the list
  • It will replace ALL of the letters in your abbreviation list, it does not know to skip anything
  • The text you paste may be not be in the pretty columns like you copied it, that's okay
  • This functionality is experimental, and may have undesired results. Use at your own risk
